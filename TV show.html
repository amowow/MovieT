The walking dead

"The Walking Dead" is a post-apocalyptic horror TV series about a group of survivors trying to stay alive in a world overrun by zombies. It follows the story of Rick Grimes, a former police officer, and explores themes of survival, morality, and the human condition in the face of extreme adversity. The show has aired for over 10 seasons and has spawned numerous spin-off series.
