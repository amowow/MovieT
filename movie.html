300 rise of an empire

300: Rise of an Empire" is a 2014 action film directed by Noam Murro and produced by Zack Snyder. It is a sequel to the 2006 film "300," and is based on the comic series "Xerxes" by Frank Miller. The film takes place before, during, and after the events of the first movie, and follows the Greek general Themistocles as he leads the charge against the invading Persian forces, led by the ruthless naval commander Artemisia. The film features stunning visual effects and epic battle sequences, and stars Sullivan Stapleton, Eva Green, Lena Headey, and Rodrigo Santoro reprising his role as Xerxes.
